About

Description

Franz Moritz Wilhelm Marc was a German painter and printmaker, one of the key figures of German Expressionism. He was a founding member of Der Blaue Reiter, a journal whose name later became synonymous with the circle of artists collaborating in it. His mature works mostly are animals, and are known for bright colors. Wikipedia
Born: 8 February 1880, Munich, Germany
Died: 4 March 1916 (age 36years), Braquis, France
Periods: Expressionism, Der Blaue Reiter, Modern art, Cubism
Parents: Wilhelm Marc, Sophie Marc
Spouse: Maria Marc (m. 19131916)
Siblings: Paul Marc
